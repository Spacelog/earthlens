What is Earth Lens?

For over forty years, astronauts have orbited Earth, looking down and admiring their home from space, taking photos along the way.

Earth Lens takes these photos - published by NASA - and curates them, letting you easily see the best photos from different missions.

The team behind Earth Lens

Earth Lens is an entirely volunteer effort, and was built by a core team of four people, with many others helping to rate and classify the thousands of photos.

The core team

Russ Garret, Alex Lee, Andrew Godwin & Mazz Mosley

Contributors

Made on an island

Earth Lens is a /dev/fort project, meaning that it was created in just one week, in isolation, on the Isle of Eigg in Scotland.
